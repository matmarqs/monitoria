\section{Modelo de Hubbard}

$$
\S_i \vdot \S_j = -\frac{1}{2} \sum_{\s, \s'} c_{i\s}^\d c_{j\s'}^\d c_{i\s'} c_{j\s} - \frac{1}{4} n_i n_{j}.
$$

Temos então que para $i=j$:
$$
\S_i^2 = -\frac{1}{2} \sum_{\s,\s'} c_{i\s}^\d n_{i\s'} c_{i\s} - \frac{1}{4} n_i^2.

$$
